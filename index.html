Surface Finishes

Plating Options

Choose the finish that suits your style. Each plating transforms the same silver piece into a unique statement.

Oxidised Finish

A dark antique patina that deepens the detail of carved and filigree work — the most traditional and sought-after look in Indian silver jewellery.

Rhodium Plating

A bright, mirror-like platinum-white finish that resists tarnish and gives jewellery a contemporary, high-shine appearance for modern wear.

Gold Finish

A warm gold tone applied over 925 silver — delivering the richness of gold jewellery at accessible pricing with excellent durability.
